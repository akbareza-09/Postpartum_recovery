Safety First

Always consult your healthcare provider before beginning any exercise program postpartum.

Stop immediately if you experience:

  • Pain or severe discomfort
  • Heaviness or bulging in your pelvis
  • Increased bleeding or new bleeding
  • Coning or doming along your midline (abdomen)
